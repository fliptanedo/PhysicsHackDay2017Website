Rules and FAQ

Rules

In order to make Hack Day fair for all, there are a few rules.

  1. Teams are limited to four members.
      If you do not have a team, we will pair you up with other members.
  2. Projects have to be made from scratch.
      In order to make the event fair, everyone needs to start from a clean slate.
      Ideas can be thought of off site. If you think you're going to need hardware for your project, feel free to bring them!
  3. Don't be a jerk.
      We have a code of conduct that all attendees must follow. The Physics Hack Day team wants everyone to be comfortable during the event. If there are any problems then we will take the necessary action to fix the problem.

FAQ

  • What should I bring?
    A laptop with a charger. You may also want to bring a toothbrush+toothpaste, and toiletries as necessary.
  • Are you locking us in?
    Participants are free to come and go as they please, but know that the best way to participate is in person!
