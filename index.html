UCR Physics Hack Day 2017

7:00 pm Friday, May 12, 2017 - 5:30 pm Saturday, May 13th, 2017; UCR Physics Building

Bridging Programming and Scientists

UCR Physics Hack Day is a 24 hour event where participants will collaborate with the goal of discovering new and creative ways to interact, manipulate, and display particle physics data sets. In the future we hope to open UCR Physics Hack Day to other schools in Southern California.

Event Information

Hackathons are 24-hour team-based challenges to rapidly prototype hardware and  software. Our hackathon will focus on scientific datasets and finding ways to make that
science more accessible to the public.
An example would be; Science Hack Day San Francisco .

Our goal is to make UCR Physics Hack Day the premier physics hackathon in Southern California. In the future, we hope to host students from other colleges along with our students here. Essentially our goal is to create an event that people can look forward to every year.
